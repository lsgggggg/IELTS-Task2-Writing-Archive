题型1（双边讨论）× 教育

题型1 教育

开头段模板

There is an ongoing debate about [Topic]. Some people think [View A], while others believe [View B]. This essay will discuss both perspectives before presenting my own viewpoint.

主体段结构

主体段1（论述 View A）：
On the one hand, it is understandable why some people think [View A]. The main reason is that [Reason 1 + explanation]. For example, [Example]. Also, [Reason 2 + explanation].

主体段2（论述 View B + 倾向性）：
On the other hand, I believe that [View B] is also important. First, [Reason 1 + explanation]. Secondly, [Reason 2 + explanation]. In addition, [Reason 3 / supplementary point].

结尾段模板

In conclusion, although [View A] can [benefit], [View B] still has its own value. I believe [balanced position] to help [target group] achieve holistic development.

题型3（同意与否）× 生活娱乐

题型3 生活娱乐

开头段模板

It is often argued that [题目核心观点的改写]. From my perspective, I fully agree with this statement, and the following essay will elaborate on the reasons for my view.
注意：题型3要求表达程度（fully/strongly/partially），不能只说 agree/disagree

主体段结构

主体段1（核心论点 + 举例）：
First and foremost, it is undeniable that [核心论点]. To be more specific, [具体解释]. For instance, [举例论证]. [补充说明/与题目关键词衔接].

主体段2（次要论点 + 正反对比）：
Secondly, another compelling reason is that [次要论点]. This is largely because [原因解释]. In contrast, [反面论证].

结尾段模板

In conclusion, I believe [重申立场], even if [让步/补充条件]. Developing this [habit/awareness] will not only help people [好处1], but also offer them [好处2].
